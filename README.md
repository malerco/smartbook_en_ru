# SmartBook

The smartest way to learn a language through real reading. Add your own books in fb2 or epub format and enjoy comfortable parallel translation without switching between apps.

## Main Features:
### Parallel Paragraph Translation 
Swipe left on any paragraph to instantly see its accurate translation.
### Instant Word Translator 
Tap any word to get translation and pronunciation.
### Smart Vocabulary 
All looked-up words are automatically saved. Study them later with intelligent flashcards using spaced repetition.
### Personal Word List 
Full list of saved words with transcription and translations.
### Comfortable Reader 
Dark mode, adjustable font size, line spacing and clean design for long reading sessions.
### Your Library 
Add and organize your own fb2 and epub books. Continue reading from where you stopped.
